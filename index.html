Common Questions

What if my bid deadline is in 48 hours?

Tier 1 is designed for 24-hour turnaround. If you're under extreme time pressure, reach out directly—we'll see what we can do.

Do you provide actual tonnage takeoffs, or just verify mine?

Tier 1 and 2 verify your existing takeoff. Tier 3 can include fresh takeoff if needed—we'll clarify scope during intake.

What format do you need the plans in?

PDF is preferred. If you have access to a plan room link (Procore, BuildingConnected, etc.), that works too.

Can I use this service repeatedly?

Absolutely. Many clients use Tier 1 on every bid over a certain dollar threshold. If you're bidding regularly, ask about monthly retainer pricing.

What if you find an error in my bid?

That's the point. We flag it, explain it, and recommend how to address it. You decide whether to adjust pricing, add qualifications, or accept the risk.

Do you keep my bid information confidential?

Yes. All plan sets, pricing, and project details are confidential. We don't share your information with anyone.
